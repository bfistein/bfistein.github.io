Information

Here's all you need to know.

FAQ

Because we know you'd ask.

Until when do I have to RSVP?

Please let us know if you can make it by March 1.

Accommodation

The venue doesn't provide any accommodation. However, it should be easily accessible from wherever in Prague you're staying.

Dress Code

Coctail attire. Color: 50 shades of blue. Ladies: please don't wear white (unless you want Angie to kill you) and we recommend not wearing stilettos. Guys: no shorts, jorts, sneakers, socks in sandals.

Photos & videos

We kindly ask you to limit your picture/video taking during the ceremony. Feel free to selfie away afterwards. And please don't forget to use the hashtag #Bengie2020 :)

Can I bring my +1?

As much as we'd love to invite everyone, our venue unfortunately has limited seating. Therefore, we kindly ask you to bring your SO only if we specifically invited both of you. Children are welcome.

Kind Requests

Please do not kidnap the wife - the groom might not come looking for her. Please do not bring your own alcohol - the venue doesn't allow it and there will be plenty. We'll have to tone it down after 10pm.

Food & Drinks

You'll be greeted with some snacks and a welcome drink. Buffet style dinner will start at 4pm and you'll have your assigned seats. Complimentary beer & wine will be served all wedding long.
