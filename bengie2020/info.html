FAQ

Because we know you'd ask.

What time do I have to be there?

Please arrive by 1pm at the latest.

How do I get there?

We recommend taking an Uber. You could also take bus 363 from Opatov and get off at Pruhonice, Komarov - but do you really want to?

Accomodation

The venue doesn't provide any accommodation. However, it should be easily accessible from wherever in Prague you're staying.

Dress Code

Business casual. Theme: blue. Ladies: please don't wear white (unless you want to get beheaded by Angie) and we recommend not wearing sharp heels. No shorts, jorts, sneakers, sandals, white socks or Manchester United jerseys (just use common sense).

Photos & videos

We kindly ask you to limit your picture/video taking during the ceremony. Feel free to selfie away afterwards.

Can I bring my +1?

Unfortunately, our venue has limited seating. Therefore, we kindly ask you to bring your SO only if we specifically invited both of you. Children are welcome.

Until when do I have to RSVP?

Please let us know if you can make it by March 1. If you end up cancelling on us, Angie will hunt you down and do case studies with you until your ears bleed.

Gift Policy

We have all the things we need. If you'd like to bring us a present, we would most appreciate a contribution to our honeymoon.

Parking

There should be plenty of parking spots right by the venue. You better not be drinking if you drive!

Rules

Do not kidnap the wife. The groom might not come looking for her. Do not bring your own alcohol - the venue doesn't allow it and there will be plenty. We'll have to tone down after 10pm.

Venue

The wedding will take place at Restaurace Hlíněná Bašta.
